About me

Ops brain,
builder mindset.

I'm Aidan. I work in operations at Rula Health, a fully remote mental health startup. Before that: Bain consultant, strategy roles at Stitch Fix, Stanford MBA. I've spent most of my career figuring out why something is slow and then fixing it.

The AI building started as part of my actual job. I'm responsible for AI adoption at Rula, so I had to learn what these tools could really do. Turns out: a lot. I started building my own tools outside work too, mostly because it's genuinely fun to make things that didn't exist before. This portfolio is what I've shipped so far.

My Journey

How I got here.

2013 Education

Indiana University, Kelley School of Business

BS in Business with Honors, Highest Distinction

Kelley Scholar — 1 of 9 students to receive a full merit-based scholarship with direct admission to the honors program. Majored in Finance and International Business. Graduated with Highest Distinction.

2013 – 2016 Work

Bain & Company

Associate Consultant → Senior Associate Consultant

Strategy consulting across industrials, agriculture, and telecom. Worked on churn analysis, org design, zero-based budgeting, and M&A. Where I learned to structure a problem fast and communicate clearly under pressure.

2016 – 2019 Work

Stitch Fix

Strategy Associate → Strategy Manager

Led a large cross-functional effort to launch Extras, Stitch Fix's first major shift toward direct sales, away from the stylist model. Built the business case, defined go-to-market strategy, and owned operations & execution through launch. Worked on business cases & launches of Plus, Kids, and UK.

2019 – 2021 Education

Stanford Graduate School of Business

MBA

LOWKeynotes — 1 of 29 students selected to develop a keynote-style talk, delivered and shared on Stanford's YouTube channel. Interned at HealthRhythms, where I built the initial pitch deck for their seed round.

2021 – 2022 Work

Serent Capital

Vice President, Growth Operations

Growth operations strategy for private equity portfolio companies. Helped management teams diagnose inefficiencies and build scalable ops infrastructure.

2022 – Now Work

Rula Health

Director of Credentialing → Director of Provider Strategy → GM, New Verticals → Head of Operations

Built the credentialing and onboarding infrastructure underpinning Rula's >20K providers nationwide. Stood up Psych provider supply operations across Marketing, Sales, Credentialing, Onboarding, Success, and Support. Launched and ran Rula's in-person therapy business. Now driving company-wide AI adoption and operational efficiency as Head of Operations.
